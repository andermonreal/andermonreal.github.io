Hi, I'm **Ander Monreal**, a cybersecurity enthusiast focused on offensive
security.

This site is my personal collection of **writeups and technical notes** —
mostly [HackTheBox](https://www.hackthebox.com/) machines, CTF challenges and
the occasional deep-dive into a tool or technique I found interesting.

Each writeup walks through the full process: reconnaissance, enumeration,
exploitation and privilege escalation, with the reasoning behind every step so
the notes are useful to read, not just to copy.

## Disclaimer

All content here is for **educational purposes** and shared only after the
related machine or challenge has been retired or solved publicly. Everything is
performed in authorized lab environments.
